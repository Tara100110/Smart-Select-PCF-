Welcome To Smart Select!

We are a project that aims to promote sustainability by allowing users to make a choice between products in the market and help them make the more informed, Eco-Friendly, and sustainable choice.

COMPARE, CHOOSE, AND CHANGE!

How Do We Do It?

A product carbon footprint (or PCF) is the calculation of all the greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions generated in the supply chain of a specific product. This is the method we rely on for calculating the carbon emissions to allow users to compare and make choices.
