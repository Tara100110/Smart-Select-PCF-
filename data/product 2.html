Swan Soap Data

Raw Material Extraction:
  • Ingredients:Ingredients: Palm Oil, Synthetic Fragrance, Sodium Lauryl Sulfate (SLS), Glycerin.
  • Estimated Emissions:
  • Palm Oil: 0.8 kg CO₂e per kg
  • Synthetic Fragrance: 1.0 kg CO₂e per kg
  • Sodium Lauryl Sulfate: 0.6 kg CO₂e per kg
  • Glycerin: 0.4 kg CO₂e per kg

    • Assuming the following quantities for a batch of 1000 soaps (150g each)

  • Palm Oil: 40 kg
  • Synthetic Fragrance: 10 kg
  • Sodium Lauryl Sulfate: 20 kg
  • Glycerin: 15 kg

Total Emisions for Raw Materials:
Total=(40×0.8)+(10×1.0)+(20×0.6)+(15×0.4)=32+10+12+6=60 kg CO e

Manufacturing:
  • Energy Use: Assume energy consumption results in emissions of about 1.5 kg CO₂e per soap.

    • Total Emissions for Manufacturing:
    Total=1000×1.5=1500 kg CO e

Packaging:

    Materials: plastic wrap and cardboard box

  • Estimated Emissions:
  • Plastic Wrap: 0.3 kg CO₂e per soap
  • Cardboard Box: 0.2 kg CO₂e per box

    • Assuming each box contains ten soaps:

  • Total boxes needed: 100

    • Total emissions from packaging:


    Total=(1000×0.3)+(100×0.2)=300+20=320 kg CO e

Distribution:
  • Transport Method: Truck transport over an average distance of 800 km.
  • Estimated Emissions: Assume transport emissions are about 0.15 kg CO₂e per soap.

    • Total emissions from Distribution:
    Total=1000×0.15=150 kg CO e

Use Phase:
  • Consumer Use: Average use of one bar lasts for about two months with daily use.
  • Estimated Emissions:Assume emissions from water heating and other factors are around 0.02 kg CO₂e per wash. Assuming one wash daily for two months (60 washes):

    • Total emissions from usage:
    Total=60×0.02=1.2 kg CO e

Disposal:
  • End-of-Life Scenario: Assuming the soap is composted or disposed of in a landfill.
  • Estimated Emissions: Assume emissions from disposal are around 0.1 kg CO₂e per soap.

    • Total emissions from disposal:
    Total=1000×0.1=100 kg CO e

Overall Carbon Footprint Calculation

Now, summing all the emissions from each stage:
Total Carbon Footprint= Raw Materials+ Manufacturing + Packaging + Distribution + Use Phase + Disposal
=60+1500+320+150+1.2+100=2131.2 kg CO e
