Shimmer blush makeup Data

Raw Material Extraction:
  • Ingredients: Assume the blush contains natural pigments, oils, and preservatives.

    • Estimated Emissions:

  • Natural pigments: 0.4 kg CO2e per kg (lower due to sustainable sourcing)
  • Oils: 0.2 kg CO2e per kg
  • Preservatives: 0.1 kg CO2e per kg
  • Total Raw Material Weight: 1 kg

Total Emisions for Raw Materials:

Total=(0.4+0.2+0.1)×1 kg=0.7 kg CO2e

Manufacturing:
  • Processes: Mixing, filling, packaging.
  • Energy consumption (electricity, gas): 1.5 kg CO2e per unit produced (more efficient processes).

    Total Manufacturing Emissions=Total=1.5 kg CO2e

Packaging:
  • Materials Used: Assume the blush is packaged in a recycled plastic container with a cardboard box.
  • Estimated Emissions:
  • Recycled plastic container: 0.8 kg CO2e per unit (lower due to recycling)
  • Cardboard box: 0.05 kg CO2e per unit

    • Total Packaging Emissions=(0.8+0.05)=0.85 kg CO2e

Distribution:
  • Transport Mode: Trucking to retailers.
  • Distance: Assume an average distance of 200 km.

    • Carbon Emissions: Adjusted for packaging weight (assuming total product weight is now 1 kg + packaging weight of approximately 0.3 kg):


  • Total weight for transport =1.3kg

    • Transportation emissions: 0.08 kg CO2e km×(200 km)=16 kg CO2e
    Adjusted for weight factor=(16)+(6)=22 kg CO2e

Cooking and Use Phase:
  • Consumer Usage: Assume average usage leads to emissions from packaging waste and product application.
  • Carbon Emissions: Packaging waste post consumer =0.3 kg CO2e

    • Total Consumption Emissions=0.3 kg CO2e

Disposal:
  • Disposal Method: Landfill with some recycling.
  • Carbon Emissions: Landfill emissions=0.5 kg CO2e

    • Total Disposal Emissions=0.5 kg CO2e

Overall Carbon Footprint Calculation

Now, summing all the emissions from each stage:

Total Carbon Footprint= Raw Materials+ Manufacturing + Packaging + Distribution + Use Phase and Cooking + Disposal

Thus, the total cradle-to-grave carbon footprint for a unit of blush 'Shimmer' is approximately 26.8 kg CO2e.
